About Us 

VR Solutions was founded in 2018 with the idea that 3d mapping would be beneficial to schools regarding school safety, more specifically, first responders.
While our focus is the K-12 setting. We acknowledge that this technology can be utilized in a variety of ways. we offer custom plans for a variety of industries to suit your needs.

K12 Safety and Security 

Our school safety package includes complete coverage of your building. We scan every room, hallway, kitchen, bathroom and closet. It is out most comprehensive package we offer. We have the ability to label almost any item, such as safe rooms, exits, and priority locations to be paired with a school-based safety plan.

In general these plans are used during safety planning meetings as well as given to first responders in order to learn the ins and outs of a building before an event takes place.

These package includes a floor plan view, a dollhouse view that can be minuplated to see different angles, and a 360 tour of the building.

Price is based off total sq footage of the project. For more information and for an up-close look at our work. Please contact us for a demonstration.
