VR Solutions

VR Solutions specializes in virtural reality mapping for small and large scale businesses and buildings. 

About Us 

VR Solutions was founded in 2018 with the idea that 3d mapping would be beneficial to schools regarding school safety, and more specifically, first responders.
While our focus is the K-12 setting, we acknowledge that this technology can be utilized in a variety of ways. We offer custom plans for a variety of industries to suit your needs.

K12 Safety and Security 

     Our school safety package includes complete coverage of your building. We scan every room, hallway, kitchen, bathroom and closet. It is the most comprehensive package we offer. We have the ability to label almost any item or location, such as safe rooms, exits, and priority areas. This package would ideally be paired with a school-based safety plan.

      In general, these plans are used during safety planning meetings as well as being provided to first responders, in order to learn the ins-and-outs of a building before a crisis event takes place.

     This package includes a floor plan view, a dollhouse view that can be minuplated to see different angles, and a 360 tour of the building.

      Pricing is calculated by total square footage of the project. For more information or to request an in-person demonstration, please contact us to schedule a meeting. 

School Promotion

     Help promote your school by giving parents, community, potential students, and employees the chance to view your facilities. Our school promotion includes 360 degree photos and VR tours that are intended to be shared via social media and are made available to the public. 

      Our school promotion package is custom made to fit your needs. We work with our clients to develop an individual plan to meet your goals. Pricing varies and is based on project needs.

Business Package 

     Our business package allows clients to showcase their business to potential customers. We can provide an interactive VR expierence that can be viewed in either a VR headset or through standard smartphone. 
     Potential clients can view your business and interact with virtural menus or inventory. These tours can be shared on social media.
